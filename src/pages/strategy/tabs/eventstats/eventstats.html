What's this page for?
This page scans your past event records and generates a showcase based on your performance.
What does "Use Database Information" do?
Instead of using snapshots during your event progress, "Use Database Information" scans all sorties made during your event period, allowing for more data to be obtained and displayed. Scanning your data also allows for showcases of prior events before XX-XX-XXXX to be generated. However, enabling this feature may result in longer load times.
